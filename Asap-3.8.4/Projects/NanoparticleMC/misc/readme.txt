This folder contains:

colatomsbyc.py: Colors atoms by their coordination number.

readsmc.py: reads the number of configurations(counts) of a surface monte carlo.

plot_EvsI_mc.py: plot the energy versus the step number for a given atoms monte carlo.

saveenergiesamc_end.py: saves the energies for plot_EvsI_mc.py for an atom monte carlo.

viewatomsmc.py: view a configuration from the amc files.

viewCluster: view a smc cluster.
